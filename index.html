Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Direct responses regarding project estimation, licensing constraints, and contract types optimized for snippet answers.

How does ApexVertex handle the project estimation and budgeting process?

Our project estimation protocol uses highly accurate Building Information Modeling (BIM) calculations coupled with current local material indices. We provide clients with a detailed, line-item budget proposal within 10-14 business days.

Is ApexVertex licensed and insured to operate in Texas and Georgia?

Yes, ApexVertex Construction is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We hold active commercial general contractor certifications in Texas and Georgia.

What is the difference between Design-Build and General Contracting?

Design-Build is a unified delivery method where ApexVertex manages both architectural design and construction under a single contract. General Contracting separates design and construction execution.
