Our Identity

Engineering Trust, Building Futures

Founded in 2001, ApexVertex Construction has grown from a local Texas engineering consultancy into one of the nation's leading commercial general contractors, managing over $1.2B in development.

Our Story

Building with Integrity & Precision

ApexVertex Construction was founded on a simple principle: combine traditional structural craftsmanship with cutting-edge engineering tech. Over the past 25 years, we have expanded our operations to major commercial hotspots in Houston, Austin, Dallas, and Atlanta.

Our Design-Build approach integrates architectural schematics, structural engineering, and actual construction work under a single point of accountability. This minimizes friction, eliminates costly clashes, and ensures projects finish on time.

What Guides Us

Our Four Core Values

Innovation
